李昌(Chang Li)

 

Cheers! I'm a third year PhD student at ILPS, University of Amsterdam, UvA, supervised by Prof. Dr. Maarten de Rijke. I focus on online learning to rank, particularly on Multi-Armed Bandits. Before joining ILPS, I received my bachelor degree from TJU in 2013. My mentor was Dr. Xin Wang. Then I received my master degree from USTC, supervised by Prof. Dr. Huanhuan Chen in 2016. You may find more about me in my CV.
