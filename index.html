李昌(Chang Li)

 

Cheers! I'm a second year PhD student in ILPS, University of Amsterdam, UvA, supervised by Prof. Dr. Maarten de Rijke. Before joining in ILPS, I received my bachelor degree from TJU in 2009. My mentor was Dr. Xin Wang. Then I received my master degree from USTC, supervised by Prof. Dr. Huanhuan Chen in 2013. My research interests are information retrieval, machine learning and bandits. Meanwhile I'm open to new challenges at all time.
